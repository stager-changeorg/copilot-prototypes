Starter Co-Pilot
Prototypes

Two hypotheses for an AI-powered campaign dashboard. Each explores a different model for how the co-pilot should guide petition starters.

Victory Paths
Victory Paths
Choose a campaign strategy upfront. The co-pilot builds a structured, phase-based roadmap from your selection.
Onboarding via 3 path cards — Quick Win, Coalition Play, Long Game
Half-dial signature tracker with milestone celebrations and confetti
Phase stepper (Spark → Build → Pressure → Decision) — clickable
Checkable next steps + collapsible full roadmap
Real-time signal alerts as dismissible banners
Open prototype → victory-paths.html
Co-Pilot
Conversational Co-Pilot
Onboarding through a 5-question chatbot. The co-pilot lives in a persistent chat and guides the campaign conversationally.
5-question onboarding chatbot with typing indicators and quick replies
Persistent chat fills the home screen — co-pilot responds contextually
Signals surface as co-pilot chat messages with inline quick replies
Full roadmap as a separate collapsible screen with expandable tasks
Personalized opening plan built from onboarding answers
Open prototype → copilot-chat.html
