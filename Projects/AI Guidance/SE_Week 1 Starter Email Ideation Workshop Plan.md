# **Workshop Plan:**  **Week 1 Starter Email Journey**

## Building an onboarding email experience that builds confidence, momentum, and clarity for Starters

# **Introduction**

The first few days after someone launches a petition on Change.org are some of the most emotionally charged and pivotal moments in their journey. Starters often publish with a surge of excitement—motivated by purpose but unsure of what comes next. Without clear next steps, encouragement, and support, that early energy can quickly fade. In fact, **66% of petition Starters never return to their dashboard after publishing**.

Our current onboarding emails aren’t doing enough to bring Starters back or help them understand the tools available to succeed. Engagement is low—**Day 0 open rates are just 30% with a 8-9% clickthrough. The very next day drops to an 18% open rate and a 4% CTR**. This leaves a critical gap in our ability to reinforce early wins, provide guidance, and build confidence during a starter’s most vulnerable window.

This workshop will focus on crafting a thoughtful, emotionally resonant email onboarding experience for the first 7 days of a petition’s life. Grounded in the emotional arc of the Starter journey and lifecycle marketing data, our goal is to design emails that drive **return visits, sharing activity, and sustained momentum**—ultimately helping more Starters feel confident, supported, and successful.

## **Pre-workshop goals**

Discuss today: Brainstorm exact behaviors we want to drive

* Define behavioral outcomes we want to drive (e.g., download the app, use ai recs)

* Define emotional outcomes we want to drive

* Promising email topics and triggers based on above for Build-a-Bear activity

## **Primary workshop goal**

* Co-create a strategic direction for the first 7 days of the Starter onboarding email journey

  * Output: A rough framework of content and sequence of emails for MVP

    * **Standard** emails everyone gets at a **predetermined cadence** (e.g., download the app)

    * **Personalized triggered** emails based on petition activity (e.g., signature milestones)

    * *Said simply, everyone gets standardized emails throughout their first week that set them up for success. During that first week,  if you are hitting milestones and getting comments, you will also get a triggered email.*

## **Secondary workshop goals**

* Use the team’s combined ideas and experience to come up with better email content than we could on our own

* Set us up for future thinking around push notifications, in-product notifications, and “Next Steps” in-product

* Make sure everyone—PD and LCM—is aligned on the vision and scope, build shared ownership and excitement so the whole team is invested in what comes next
